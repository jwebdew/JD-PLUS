How Our Story Begins

Shake Shack sprouted from a hot dog cart in Madison Square Park in
Manhattan to support the Madison Square Park Conservancy’s first art
installation. The cart was quite the success, with Shack fans lined up
daily for three summers. In 2004, a permanent kiosk opened
in the park: Shake Shack was born.

Stand For Something Good

  • Good Ingredients
  • Good 'n' Green
  • Good Neighbors
  • ABOUT THE BEEF
    Our custom blend of freshly ground (never frozen!) 100% Angus beef, made from premium whole muscle cuts —with no hormones or antibiotics, ever—is the founding DNA of our menu.
    MARTIN'S POTATO ROLLS
    The best thing since sliced bread: all buns in all Shacks worldwide are non-GMO and made by our potato-y pals at Martin’s Potato Rolls. P.S. They’ve got gluten-free buns, too!
    FROZEN CUSTARD
    Don’t call it ice cream: our dense, rich, and creamy frozen custard is the real deal. Our recipes only use real sugar, cage-free eggs, and milk from dairy farmers who pledge not to use artificial growth hormones
  • SHACK2O®
    We’re a proud member of 1% for the Planet, which means 1% of every SHACK2O® bottle sold supports the Waterkeeper Alliance and the cleanup of water sources around the globe.
    SUSTAINABLE AGRICULTURE
    Through partners like Niman Ranch, we’re committed to supporting ranches and farms designed to have minimal negative impact on the environment.
    SOLAR ROOFS
    Raise the roof! Our King of Prussia, PA; The Domain, Austin; and Westbury, Long Island Shacks feature solar roofs that each produce about 20,400kWh/year, or the average consumption of two average-size single homes.
  • SHACK TRACK & FIELD™
    Our free community fitness program is open to all ages and abilities, and the first drink is on us after every event. Hit the streets with Shack!
    LOCAL ARTISANAL FOOD PARTNERS
    Each Shack features a selection of local food partners and purveyors, from Uchi in Austin and Publican Quality Meats in Chicago to Four & Twenty Blackbirds in Brooklyn and Dandelion Chocolate in the Bay Area.
    JOBS
    At Shake Shack, we take care of each other first and foremost so that we’re set up to succeed for our guests, community, suppliers, and investors.
